Wondering around is a unique experience especially if the it's through woods. The mountain ranges in Kerala consisting of the highland area of the Western Ghats, exude an exotic charm.

Woods not only offer us view but also memories that can become a never ending story. This site is not about the story but a note written by someone on his journey throught dreams
